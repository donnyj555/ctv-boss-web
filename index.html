Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I make a TV ad?

Under a minute. Paste a Zillow URL, pick the photos you want, hit render. The AI handles script, voice-over, music, and the dual-format export.

Are CTV ad buys included in my subscription?

No — the subscription covers ad creation. CTV airtime is billed separately at cost, with placement managed by our team. You can create unlimited Facebook and Instagram Reels at no additional cost and post them yourself.

What's the difference between Starter and Pro?

Starter is $27/mo with 2 CTV ads per cycle and a small "Created with CTVBoss" watermark. Pro is $67/mo with unlimited renders, no watermark, AI virtual staging for vacant rooms, and AI twilight photo enhancement.

Do you really cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account anytime — no calls, no forms. You keep your features through the end of your current billing cycle.

What if my listing isn't on Zillow?

You can paste a Realtor.com URL or upload photos directly. The AI will work from whatever photos you give it.
