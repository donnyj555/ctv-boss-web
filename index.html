Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about advertising on Connected TV.

How quickly can my business start seeing results?

Impact can be observed through increased website traffic and community buzz shortly after the ads start running. Because we use Geographic Zipcode Targeting, you establish a strong local presence immediately. We recommend monitoring for at least 3 months to gauge the full compounding effect.

If I'm a small business, don't I need to spend a lot to create a commercial?

Not at all! Unlike other platforms that expect you to shoot and edit the ad yourself, our team provides custom, broadcast-quality 30-second commercial creation for a one-time fee of just $495. This instantly gives your local brand a premium, national-level aesthetic without the massive agency production costs.

On which channels will my ads be displayed?

Our focus is on targeting the people watching, not just the channel. However, through our inventory partners, your ads will stream alongside premium content on networks like Hulu, Peacock, ESPN, Discovery, HGTV, A&E, and live local news apps.

What is the cost of advertising, and what does it include?

We price based on Completed Views. With the CTVBOSS banner discount, rates start at an incredible $49.90 per 1,000 views. In a small zip code, you can reach thousands of connected TVs multiple times a week for just $499/month. We charge a $139/month management fee for Local Saturation campaigns, or a 15% agency fee for custom budgets, to handle the entire campaign, including on-demand detailed reporting.
