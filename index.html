Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about advertising on Connected TV.

How quickly can my business start seeing results?

Impact can be observed through increased website traffic and community buzz shortly after the ads start running. Because we use Geographic Zipcode Targeting, you establish a strong local presence immediately. We recommend monitoring for at least 3 months to gauge the full compounding effect.

If I'm a single agent, don't I need to spend a lot to shoot a commercial?

Not at all! Unlike other platforms that expect you to hire a camera crew, our CTV HOMES platform automatically creates your commercial pulling directly from your Zillow listing. With real voice-over, music, and professional transitions, it gives your brand a premium TV aesthetic instantly without the massive production costs.

On which channels will my ads be displayed?

Our focus is on targeting the people watching, not just the channel. However, through our inventory partners, your ads will stream alongside premium content on networks like Hulu, Peacock, ESPN, Discovery, HGTV, A&E, and live local news apps.

What is the cost of advertising, and what does it include?

We price based on Completed Views. With the CTVBOSS banner discount, rates start at an incredible $49.90 per 1,000 views. In a small zip code, you can reach thousands of connected TVs multiple times a week for just $499/month. We charge a 15% agency fee for custom budgets, to handle the entire campaign, including on-demand detailed reporting.
