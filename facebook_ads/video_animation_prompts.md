# AI Video Generation Prompts for Facebook Ads

This document provides prompt ideas outling how you can animate the static Facebook ad images using tools like Runway Gen-2, Luma Dream Machine, or Kling AI.

These prompts are designed to add subtle, high-quality motion that catches the eye without looking artificial or distracting from the core message.

---

## Image 1: `facebook_ad_hulu_family.png`
**Goal:** Warmth, engagement, and the glow of the television.

* **Prompt Idea 1 (Subtle):** *"Cinematic slow motion. A family sits on a couch at dusk. The warm, flickering light of a television screen reflects gently on their faces. The father slightly turns his head to smile at the child. High photorealism."*
* **Prompt Idea 2 (Dynamic):** *"Soft focal shift. The camera slowly pushes in towards the family. The child points excitedly at the off-screen television. Cozy living room atmosphere, sharp focus on the faces."*

## Image 2: `facebook_ad_remote_closeup.png`
**Goal:** Actionable, focusing on the moment of choice.

* **Prompt Idea 1 (Subtle):** *"Macro shot, shallow depth of field. A hand holding a TV remote control. The thumb presses the 'OK' button, and a soft blue LED light flashes on the remote. Subtle background blur."*
* **Prompt Idea 2 (Dynamic):** *"First-person perspective. The hand slowly raises the remote towards the screen. The background room lighting slightly dims as if a movie is about to start. High detail on the texture of the remote."*

## Image 3: `facebook_ad_real_estate.png`
**Goal:** Professionalism and aspiration.

* **Prompt Idea 1 (Subtle):** *"A professional real estate agent stands in a bright, modern living room. She smiles warmly and gently nods her head. Natural sunlight streams through the window in the background, dust motes practically visible. 4k resolution."*
* **Prompt Idea 2 (Dynamic):** *"Slow panning shot. The camera pans from left to right across the beautiful living room, ending on the real estate agent who gives a welcoming gesture with her hands. Bright, airy, and inviting."*

## Image 4: `facebook_ad_home_improvement.png`
**Goal:** Trust, quality, and transformation.

* **Prompt Idea 1 (Subtle):** *"A confident contractor in a renovated kitchen. He adjusts his hard hat slightly and smiles at the camera. Soft, natural lighting. High photorealism, emphasizing the clean lines of the new kitchen behind him."*
* **Prompt Idea 2 (Dynamic):** *"Time-lapse feel (but normal speed). The contractor stands proudly in the foreground while the lighting in the kitchen subtly shifts from morning to afternoon light, highlighting the quality of the work."*

## Image 5: `facebook_ad_sports.png`
**Goal:** Excitement and high energy.

* **Prompt Idea 1 (Subtle):** *"A couple sitting in a home theater environment, intensely watching a sports game off-screen. They suddenly react with excitement, throwing their arms up in celebration. Dynamic lighting from the TV screen."*
* **Prompt Idea 2 (Dynamic):** *"Slow motion reaction shot. The couple leaps off the couch cheering. Popcorn flies slightly in the air. High contrast, vibrant colors, capturing the thrill of the moment."*

---

### Tips for Best Results:
1. **Start Simple:** Often, the best AI videos come from very simple prompts. Let the image do the heavy lifting!
2. **Specify Lighting:** Mentioning "flickering TV light," "cinematic lighting," or "natural sunlight" dramatically improves realism.
3. **Use the "Image + Text" feature:** Make sure you upload the image *first*, and then type your prompt to guide how the AI moves that specific image.
4. **Iterate:** AI video can be unpredictable. You may need to run the same prompt 3 or 4 times to get the perfect 4-second clip.
