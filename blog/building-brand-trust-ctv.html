Building Local Trust: Why Consumers Believe What They See on TV

By CTV HOMES | 4 min read

In the digital age of deepfakes, spam emails, and easily manipulated online reviews, consumer trust is at an all-time low. If a local homeowner is looking for a general contractor to remodel their kitchen, they are inherently skeptical of a random sponsored post on their Facebook feed.

However, despite decades of technological change, the psychological impact of television remains remarkably constant. Consumers inherently trust the brands they see on the big screen.

The Psychological "Halo Effect"

When a local business advertises on Connected TV (CTV), they instantly benefit from a psychological bias known as the Halo Effect. Because television advertising historically required massive, million-dollar budgets, the consumer subconsciously assumes that any company appearing on their TV must be highly successful, incredibly legitimate, and widely trusted.

They think, "If this local plumber can afford to run commercials on Hulu during my favorite show, they must be the best plumber in the city."

Premium Environment matters

The credibility of an advertisement is deeply tied to the environment where it lives. A social media ad is sandwiched between a chaotic mix of meme videos, political arguments, and personal updates.

A CTV ad, by contrast, lives within highly produced, premium, Hollywood-level content. Your local commercial might play immediately after an ad for Apple or Mercedes Benz, and immediately before an episode of a prestige drama. Your brand absorbs the premium nature of the content surrounding it.

The ROI of Trust

Building this level of brand trust has massive downstream effects for a local business. When that same consumer later searches Google for a plumber and sees your business name in the search results, they will bypass three competitors and click on your website, simply because they recognize your name from TV.

Your search engine marketing (SEM) costs drop, your website conversion rate rises, and your sales team closes deals faster because the prospect already trusts your brand before they ever pick up the phone.
