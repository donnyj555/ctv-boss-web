Do I Need a $10,000 Commercial to Succeed on CTV?

By CTV HOMES | 5 min read

The single biggest objection most local business owners have to television advertising isn't the cost of the media placement—it's the cost of producing the commercial itself. The industry myth is that a TV ad requires a massive production crew, actors, and a $10,000 upfront budget.

With the rise of CTV, that old-school thinking is officially dead. The barrier to entry for high-quality creative has never been lower.

Just a single high-quality photo can serve as the foundation of your commercial.

We often find that clients already sit on a goldmine of creative assets. Those pictures you took for your new website? The quick iPhone video of your team working? When handed to a professional editor who layers on robust motion graphics, professional voiceover, and high-energy music, those basic assets transform into a stunning 30-second spot.

The "Animated Explainer" Approach

If you have absolutely no visual assets, do not panic. Some of the highest-converting CTV commercials rely entirely on stock video footage or clean, modern motion graphics.

These ads clearly state the value proposition, use large readable text right on the screen, and drive viewers to a specific action (like scanning a QR code or visiting a website).
