The Rise of FAST Channels: What Advertisers Need to Know

By CTV HOMES | 5 min read

As consumers experience "subscription fatigue" from managing monthly bills for Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Max, and a dozen other services, a massive shift has occurred in the streaming landscape. Viewers are flocking to platforms that offer hundreds of channels for one unbeatable price: Free.

These platforms are known as FAST channels (Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV), and they represent one of the biggest opportunities in the history of local advertising.

What Exactly Are FAST Channels?

FAST platforms look and feel a lot like traditional cable television, complete with a programming guide showing what is currently playing live. The difference is they stream entirely over the internet, and they are completely free for the user because they are supported by advertising.

Major players in the FAST space include:

  • Pluto TV (Owned by Paramount)
  • Tubi (Owned by Fox)
  • The Roku Channel
  • Amazon Freevee
  • Samsung TV Plus

These platforms offer everything from major motion pictures and live news broadcasts to dedicated 24/7 channels for classic shows like The Price is Right or Survivor.

Why Audiences Are Hooked

Aside from the obvious benefit of being free, audiences love FAST channels because of the "lean-back" experience. Unlike navigating the overwhelming menus of Netflix trying to decide what to watch, FAST channels recreate the classic TV experience of simply flipping on the TV, finding something already playing, and relaxing.

The Opportunity for Local Advertisers

Because FAST channels are fundamentally built on advertising, they offer an incredible volume of premium inventory for marketers. Here is why local businesses should be paying attention:

1. Premium Content Adjacency

Your commercial can run during a blockbuster Sunday movie or a live CBS News broadcast on Pluto TV. The content is premium, meaning your brand looks premium by association.

2. 100% Completion Rates

Unlike YouTube, ads on FAST channels are generally unskippable. When a commercial break happens in a movie on Tubi, the viewer watches your entire 30-second spot. This guarantees your message is actually heard.

3. Highly Precise Targeting

Despite feeling like traditional TV, FAST platforms deliver ads digitally. This means you aren't buying the "Pluto TV network." You are buying the exact zip codes and demographics of the users streaming Pluto TV in your local service area.
