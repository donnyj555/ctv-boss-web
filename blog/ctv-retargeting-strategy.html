The Power of CTV Retargeting: Closing the Deal in the Living Room

By CTV HOMES | 4 min read

Imagine this: A user clicks on your Google ad, browses your website's roofing services, and then gets distracted and leaves without calling. Traditionally, you might try to hit them with a tiny banner ad on a news website the next day to remind them of your business.

But what if, instead of a banner ad that they ignore, they sat down that evening to watch Hulu, and a high-definition, 30-second commercial for your roofing business played right on their 65-inch TV?

That is the incredible power of CTV Retargeting.

How CTV Retargeting Works

CTV retargeting bridges the gap between digital action and television impact. It works seamlessly through a process called IP matching and cross-device graphing:

  1. The Visit: A user visits your website on their phone or laptop. A small piece of code (a pixel) logs the visit and notes the IP address of their household internet connection.
  2. The Match: That IP address is matched to the Connected TV devices (like a Roku or Apple TV) connected to that exact same household WiFi network.
  3. The Delivery: Later that evening, when someone in that household turns on a streaming app, the ad network recognizes the TV and delivers your commercial straight into their living room.

Why It's Disproportionately Effective

Retargeting works because it focuses your ad spend exclusively on "warm" leads—people who have already demonstrated interest in your product.

When you combine the high-intent nature of retargeting with the prestige and unskippable nature of a television commercial, the results are explosive. The consumer perceives your brand as massive and highly credible (since you are "on TV"), when in reality, you just brilliantly targeted their specific household.
