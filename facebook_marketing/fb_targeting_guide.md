# Facebook Ads Targeting Guide for CTV BOSS

To get the best return on your Facebook Ads, you need to reach the decision-makers (owners, founders, marketing directors). Below is the recommended targeting setup specifically designed for local B2B lead generation.

## 1. Core Demographic Settings
- **Location:** The specific city, county, or zip codes where you want to offer your CTV services. *Do not leave this broad.*
- **Age:** 30-65+. (Most established local business owners fall into this range).
- **Gender:** All

## 2. Detailed Targeting (Interests & Behaviors)
*You should test these in separate "Ad Sets" to see which performs best, rather than mixing them all together initially.*

### Ad Set A: The "Direct Business Owner" Audience
This targets people who Facebook explicitly knows manage business assets.
**Include Behaviors & Job Titles:**
- Facebook Page Admins (Business Page Admins)
- Small Business Owners (Behavior)
- Job Titles: Owner, Founder, CEO, Co-Founder, Managing Director, President

### Ad Set B: The "Industry Specific" Audience
If you want to niche down (which is highly recommended for higher conversion rates), target specific high-value local industries.
**Include Interests:**
- **Home Services:** Plumbing, HVAC, Roofing, Landscaping, General contractor
- **Legal/Medical:** Personal injury lawyer, Dentistry, Chiropractic, Plastic surgery
- **Real Estate:** Real estate broker, Real estate investing

### Ad Set C: The "Marketing Interested" Audience
This targets business owners who are actively trying to grow their business and are likely running ads already.
**Include Interests:**
- Local advertising
- Digital marketing
- Social media marketing
- Return on investment
- Search engine optimization

## 3. The "Narrow Audience" Strategy (Crucial)
To prevent your ads from showing to regular employees of small businesses (who have no buying power), you must use the **"Narrow Audience"** (or "Must Also Match") feature.

**Example Setup:**
- **Include:** Small Business Owners (Behavior) OR Facebook Page Admins
- **AND MUST ALSO MATCH:** Top 10% or Top 25% of Zip Codes Household Income (Optional, but helps filter out non-decision makers).
- **AND MUST ALSO MATCH:** (Your specific industry interest, like "Home Services" or "Legal").

## 4. Retargeting (The "Warm" Audience)
Once you start driving traffic from the ads above to your `index.html` landing page, you must set up a retargeting audience. Business owners rarely buy B2B services on the first click.

- **Setup:** Create a Custom Audience of "Anyone who visited your website in the last 30 days."
- **Ad Strategy:** Show this audience "Angle 3" (the curiosity angle) or social proof videos/testimonials to push them to fill out the Lead Capture form.

## 5. Budget & Testing Advice
- Start with **Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO)** turned on.
- Set a budget of **$15-$30/day** per ad set to start. Let it run for at least 3-4 days before turning anything off; Facebook's AI needs time to find the owners in your local area.
- Use **Lead Generation Objectives** (either native Facebook Lead Forms or conversions driving to your new website). Native Facebook forms often convert at a higher rate, but driving to your website yields higher *quality* leads.
