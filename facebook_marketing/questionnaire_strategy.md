# Automating the Lead Qualification Questionnaire

You have hit on the most important part of B2B lead generation! In marketing, we call this the **"Two-Step Funnel."** 

Step 1 (which we already built) is the "low-friction" capture to just get the email. 
Step 2 is the "high-friction" qualification questionnaire where you gather the intel you need to actually pitch them (phone number, website, zip code, budget). 

Here are the two best ways to fully automate this without you lifting a finger:

## Option 1: The "Instant Redirect" Funnel (Highly Recommended 🔥)
Instead of waiting to email them, we ask them the questions *immediately* while they are still excited on your website.

**How it works:**
1. A user clicks "Get My Free Strategy Focus" on your current `ctvboss.com` form.
2. I add one invisible line of code to the form that tells Formspree to **instantly redirect them** to a hidden page on your site: `ctvboss.com/step-2`
3. The `step-2.html` page says: *"Success! We're analyzing your market. Before we finalize your custom CTV strategy, tell us a bit more about your business location so we can pull the right TV network data..."*
4. Below that is a second, longer Formspree form asking for Location, Phone Number, and Business Type.

*Why this is best:* People are 5x more likely to fill out a long form if they've *already* committed to filling out a short form first. 

## Option 2: The Mailchimp Autoresponder (The "Drip" Method)
If you'd rather they leave the website and get an email later.

**How it works:**
1. They fill out the short form on `ctvboss.com`.
2. Formspree sends the email to your Mailchimp audience (like we set up earlier).
3. Inside Mailchimp, you create an "Automated Customer Journey / Welcome Email." 
4. The email automatically triggers 5 minutes later, saying: *"Hey, thanks for requesting a CTV strategy! Please fill out this quick questionnaire so I can start pulling your local data."* 
5. The email contains a link to a Typeform or Google Form.

### What should the Intel Questions be?
Regardless of which option you choose, you need to ask the right questions to qualify them. I recommend:
1. **Business Category:** (Drop-down: Home Services, Medical, Legal, Retail, Other)
2. **Target Zip Codes / City:** "Where are your ideal customers located?"
3. **Current Marketing:** "Are you currently running Facebook or Google Ads?" (Yes/No - tells you if they have a budget!)
4. **Phone Number:** "Best number to text/call with your report?"
